# knowledge.relativity.energy_momentum

**Key equations**:
```
p = mv/√(1−v²/c²)      4-momentum: p^i = (E/c, p)
E = mc²/√(1−v²/c²)     p_i p^i = m²c² → E²/c² = p² + m²c²
```

**Rest energy**: E = mc² at v=0. This is not an added postulate —
it follows from L = −mc²√(1−v²/c²) and E = p·v − L.

**Force and acceleration**: Not parallel in relativity!
F_∥ = mγ³a_∥, F_⊥ = mγa_⊥. The "longitudinal mass" mγ³ and
"transverse mass" mγ differ.

**Mass non-conservation**: The mass of a composite body m ≠ Σ m_a.
Only the total energy (including rest energies) is conserved.
Binding energy contributes to mass: nuclear, chemical, etc.

**Classical limit**: v≪c → p≈mv, E≈mc²+½mv².

- Landau Vol.2 §9
